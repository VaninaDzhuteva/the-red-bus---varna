The Red Bus is a small family-run business. This means that we know each child and his/her parents well. We pride ourselves in having good working relationships with both students and parents. Because we are small, we can give your child quality attention in the class and give immediate feedback to parents regarding their child's progress. Our teacher, Jennifer, is an experienced teacher and TEFL/TESOL qualified. She has taught children and young people from many walks of life.

Jennifer has recently studied for a Professional Development Certificate in Phonics Teaching and is currently pursuing a Masters in International Education with the University of Cumbria. She has also worked in an International School as a class teacher and as a Learning Support staff in a Special Care Unit of a Special Education School in Wales.She has also worked in an International School as a class teacher and as a Learning Support staff in a Special Care Unit of a Special Education School in Wales. Phonics instruction at The Red Bus is taught using a combination of two popular and successful UK phonics programmes. This ensures your child gets a range of stimulating activities. Jennifer works hard to stay up to date with recent research and developments in the phonics world.

Jennifer, has been involved in Growth Mindset research (see research) and believes that children should be supported and encouraged to develop grit and tenacity. Children are taught to see mistakes as opportunities to learn and grow. As such, the teaching is holistic and child-centred.

Jennifer has worked with children and young people who have multiple and profound disabilities. She worked in a special needs school in North Wales for four years as well as assisting disabled children to communicate in an orphanage in Tanzania. Over the years, Jennifer has worked alongside many health professionals. She is passionate about helping children to communicate, whatever their age or ability.
